My Algorithm Visualizer Application

During my computer science journey, I have had the opportunity to work on several different projects that have enhanced my technological skills.

A particular project that I wanted to better enhance and further perfect is ‘My Visualizer Application’. This artifact, in which I created a year ago, was one of the first tasks given to me during my internship with HCL Academy. This application visually displays the difference between a merging algorithm and a quicksort algorithm.

This application was meant to enhance my knowledge and skills with algorithms and data structures.
