My Calendar Application

During my computer science journey, I have had the opportunity to work on several different projects that have enhanced my technological skills.

A particular project that I wanted to better enhance, and further perfect was ‘My Calendar Application’. This artifact was the very first application I ever created with my current employer. As the name indicates, this is an application was set to tell time in the form of a calendar. It shows the current day, followed by the days of the week, the month and includes padding days, which are any additional days from the previous month.

This application was meant to enhance my knowledge specifically in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
